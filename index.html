Umme Amina Mannan

Umme Amina Mannan is perusing her Ph.D. in Pavement Materials and Geotechnical Engineering at the University of New Mexico, Albuquerque, NM after finishing her Masters from University of Akron, OH. Her research focuses on the fatigue damage and healing ability of asphalt binder and recycled asphalt materials. Currently, she is working on two NMDOT projects titled “Mechanical and Rheological RAP Characterization of NMDOT Mixes” and "Evaluation of Plus Grades of Performance Graded (PG) Asphalt Binder (Phase-II)". She was the first recipient of Elvidio Diniz scholarship in 2015 and Asphalt Pavement Association of New Mexico Asphalt Industry Scholarship in 2016.
